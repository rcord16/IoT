Audio Indicator

Enabling the Audio Indicator in the Antenna Alignment tool allows you to align a device's antenna using sound, without needing to look at the airOS UI.

The Audio Indicator's pitch corresponds to the received signal level: the higher the pitch and the more frequent beeping, the stronger the signal; the lower the pitch and the rarer beeping, the weaker the signal. As you rotate the antenna, the pitch and the beeping frequency will change; the best alignment is the position where the pitch is highest.
