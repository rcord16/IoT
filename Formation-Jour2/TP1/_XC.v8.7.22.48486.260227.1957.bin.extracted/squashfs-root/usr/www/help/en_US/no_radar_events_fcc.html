DFS prescan is a feature utilizing the secondary radio for DFS detection.

DFS prescan is started on system boot after management radio timeout. It runs for 10-30 minutes and waits for CAC (Channel Availability Check) time on each DFS channel. Frequencies where no radar events were detected during the CAC wait are marked with an asterix in the center frequency list above.

This iteration of the DFS prescan feature is informational only.
